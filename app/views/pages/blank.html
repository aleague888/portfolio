My Background

I am a former helicopter mechanic - turned web developer from northern Alabama. I have been fortunate to have travelled quite a bit and see a lot of adventure! Working through school, I got a contract in Charleston, SC. That's where I found out about The Iron Yard coding bootcamp and decided to give it a try. I am extremely glad I did because it's been one of the best decisions I've made! Since then, It's been a bit of a whirlwind - working with the brightest (and probably the craziest) people who make you realize that there really is no limit to what you can do!
